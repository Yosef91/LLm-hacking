The moon is high, the sky is black,
A chill runs down the old dirt track.

For Halloween is on its way.

A pumpkin grins with a glowing face.
Witches cackle, broomsticks fly,
As bats trace shapes across the sky.

While goblins giggle, losing sleep.
Black cats pounce with glowing eyes,
And owls call out with ghostly cries.

Knocking doors throughout the night.

Costumes worn both bold and proud.

A secret kept behind the veil,
Where haunted dreams and fears set sail.

To guard your home this chilling night.
For when the dark begins to creep…
It’s best to laugh before you sleep.
